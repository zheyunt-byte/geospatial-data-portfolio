# Geospatial Data Portfolio

This repository presents a curated portfolio of GIS and geospatial data science projects focused on spatial statistics, sampling design, exploratory data analysis, and environmental data interpretation. The projects were reorganized into publication-ready case studies with neutral project naming, project-specific documentation, and redacted source files.

## Featured Projects

### [Florida Aviation Elevation Analysis](projects/florida-aviation-elevation-analysis/README.md)

Spatial and descriptive analysis of aviation facility elevations in Florida, including distributional assessment and mean versus weighted mean center mapping.

### [Aviation Facility Sampling and Inference](projects/aviation-facility-sampling-and-inference/README.md)

A sampling and inference workflow comparing simple random sampling and stratified sampling for U.S. aviation facilities, with hypothesis framing for facility-type elevation differences.

### [Humidity and Thermal Comfort Analysis](projects/humidity-thermal-comfort-analysis/README.md)

Environmental time-series analysis using hourly and daily weather observations to derive vapour pressure, vapour pressure deficit, dewpoint temperature, lifting condensation level, and humidex.

### [Spatial Patterns and Inferential Analysis](projects/spatial-patterns-and-inferential-analysis/README.md)

Archived report artifact documenting a spatial analysis workflow centered on pattern interpretation and inferential reasoning.

### [Spatial Autocorrelation and Regression](projects/spatial-autocorrelation-and-regression/README.md)

Archived report artifact focused on spatial dependence, autocorrelation structure, and regression-based interpretation.
